A travel app for people who don’t love planning

This project was a design challenge where I was supposed to outline a product based on current pains travelers have. In my interviews, I found that most people dislike planning. This app was my solution.

Introduction Research Problem Iteration Prototype

About the project:

The Problem: Users were overwhelmed by the amount of work and information planning a trip takes.

The Solution: Creating an app the holds all the information necessary for planning, like hotels, attractions based on the user's budget and preference.

My Role: UX/UI Designer and Researcher

Tools: Figma, Photoshop

Personal Objective: My goal was to create a flow that was enjoyable and easy for the user.

Research

As a user researcher, I want to understand how travelers feel about planning their trips and what tools they use to research, book and budget.

What I found out:

Most users actually don’t like the process of booking trips.
They would hire an agency if they could.
They would rather have a platform that contains all the necessary information instead of having to go through multiple platforms.

Click here to see my interview transcripts.

Problem Statement

Plan My Trip was designed to make planning easier for the occasional traveler.

We have observed that the industry isn’t offering helpful tools to help the user plan as efficiently and easily, which is causing frustration for the users and a lack of trust for travel apps.

During my research, I found out that the occasional traveler has no pleasure or just doesn’t like planning their trips, they would pay for a travel agency if they could afford it. How might we improve Plan My Trip so that our customers are successful based on the number of returning users and good reviews left on our app page?

Prototyping And Testing

My main goal at this point was to test 3 flows:

  1. Onboarding
  2. Booking a Trip
  3. Checking out

After I created the user flow, it was time to create wireframes. I then asked users to test it.

Key Learning from User Test

I tested this prototype on 10 users. This allowed me to understand what was and wasn’t working on the app. I manage to decrease the amount of pages and clicks users had to go through to complete the project.
